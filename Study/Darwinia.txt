DARWINIA

There are two aspects to Darwinia, the first is setting up production facilities for usage, and the second is tactical combat using squads of units.

Each of these aspects shall be studied as if independent games, despite them being linked.





*Growth*

The game has you collect souls and have them be processed into living Darwinians, which act very much like a moving commodity, and there is immense satisfaction to be had from cleaning out areas and turning them into a population of functioning creatures.
